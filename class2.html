DESCRIPTION ABOUT THE TIME TABLE

  1. All students are required to be present in their respective classrooms before 8:30 AM, as the school day begins with the morning prayer at 8:30 AM. The prayer session concludes by 8:50 AM, following which regular academic activities commence.

  2. The timetable outlined above is designed to provide a structured and balanced academic schedule for Class VIII students. Each day consists of eight periods, with individual class durations ranging from 40 to 45 minutes. The school day begins at 9:00 AM and concludes at 3:30 PM, ensuring adequate instructional time across various subjects.
